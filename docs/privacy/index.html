Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-22

FlexiDiet (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices.

Information we collect

  • Account information (such as email and profile details you provide).
  • Nutrition and wellness data you log in the app.
  • Optional photos and food images you upload for analysis features.
  • Technical and diagnostic data needed to operate and improve the app.

How we use information

  • Provide core app functionality (tracking, recommendations, and AI features).
  • Improve feature quality, reliability, and user experience.
  • Support account management, billing, and customer support.

Data sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited data with service providers that help us operate the app (for example, hosting, analytics, authentication, and subscription infrastructure), under appropriate safeguards.

Data retention

We retain data as long as needed to provide services and comply with legal obligations. You may request account/data deletion by contacting support.

Your choices

  • You can update profile and nutrition entries within the app.
  • You can contact us to request deletion or support-related privacy actions.

Contact

For privacy requests or questions, use our support page: FlexiDiet Support.
